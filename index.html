About the Arbor Vitae Project

The Arbor Vitae Project aims to encourage communities to begin homesteading, combining church, farming, school, and construction to support self-sustaining and thriving communities.

Our mission is to provide the resources and knowledge necessary to create a network of interconnected legacies rooted in shared values and beliefs.
